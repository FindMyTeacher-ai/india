Our story

Built on one simple idea

FindMyTeacher was founded by Ayush Pandey in 2026, out of a simple belief: every student in India — in every state, in every city and small town — deserves access to a teacher who actually fits how they learn.

What started as an idea to connect local tutors with nearby students has grown into a platform built to serve both sides of the classroom: parents and students searching for the right teacher, and teachers looking for students who need them.

The process

Two paths, one classroom

Whether you're a parent looking for a tutor or a teacher looking for students, getting started takes minutes.

For Parents & Students

Get matched with a tutor

01

Tell us what you need

Pick a subject, board (CBSE, ICSE, State Board), class and your state — online or in‑person.

02

Review verified profiles

03
